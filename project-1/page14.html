Secret Ingredient in McNuggets

Ronald McDonald



The secret ingredient in McDonald's chicken nuggets, or McNuggets, is found to be Butylhydroquinone (TBHQ)! In 2016, Mcdonald's removed TBHQ from their chicken nuggets in attempt to make their food "healthier." While TBHQ is removed from the ingredients list, it is not quite removed from the nuggets because TBHQ is still in their cooking oil. This can be very dangerous as TBHQ is a man-made antioxidant that is used to preserve foods that contain fats, but it's also used in cosmetics, paints, and varnishes.
